BIO GRAPHY

About me

I'm Nagehan Abdullayeva (Akpinar), was born in 1993 in Uskudar, Istanbul Province, Turkey. Since 1999, I have studied at the "50 Years of the Republic School" secondary school. In 2010, I graduated from Sakarya University with a bachelor's degree in English language teaching.
My father Erol Hamdi oglu Akpinar is a civil engineer. My mother, Meltem Servi, is the chief manager.
I am married, my husband is an Azerbaijani citizen and I bear his surname. I have a daughter and a son.
I started my career as a singer in 2015.
In 2015, I started singing by singing the song "Bir Bahar".
The last songs that brought me success were "Var oldun" in 2016, "Bana ne" in 2017, and "Qal sene qurban" in 2018. The song "Gal sene qurban" composed by Bahram Nasibov brought me great success.
I am currently active, I have more than 1 million viewers.
